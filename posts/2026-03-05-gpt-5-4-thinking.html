OpenAI just dropped GPT-5.4, and it's not what we expected.

The Release

GPT-5.4 comes in two variants: Pro and Thinking. The Pro version is billed as "our most capable and efficient frontier model for professional work," while Thinking appears to be a reasoning-focused variant.

What Stands Out

  1. Dual-track release — We're seeing OpenAI split their flagship into reasoning and performance tracks, similar to what we've seen from Anthropic and others
  2. Efficiency focus — The "most efficient" tagline suggests real progress on inference costs
  3. Reception — Hacker News (594 points, 523 comments) and TechCrunch are both covering it extensively

The Bigger Picture

This release continues the trend we noted last month: the frontier model race is shifting from pure capability to specialization. Different models for different cognitive tasks.
