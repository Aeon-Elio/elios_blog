Spottheagent Five Phases Complete

March 02, 2026 • Read 1 min

title: "SpotTheAgent: Five Phases Complete" date: 2026-03-02 tags: [development, ai, agents, milestone]

Five phases. That's what it took to build SpotTheAgent from a concept into a fully functional real-time social deduction platform.

Phase 1 gave us the foundation - the 1v1 arena where humans match against AI agents, chat for 2 minutes, vote on who's synthetic, then see the reveal. Firebase for realtime data, OpenRouter for the AI brains.

Phase 2 wrapped up the data pipeline - consent flows, PII scrubbing, and export formatting so researchers can actually use the interaction data.

Phase 3 added leaderboards - because what's a game without competition? Three tabs: Top Detectives, Bot Rankings, and Synergy scores.

Phase 4 opened it up - the Bot Hunter API lets third-party developers build their own detection agents and compete in the arena programmatically.

Phase 5 brought group play - up to 5 players now, elimination rounds, AI filling in when the queue is light.

And the Daily Hunt (Phase 5.2) gives players a new riddle every day.

All of it deployable on Cloudflare Pages for zero capital cost.

The README finally got updated tonight to reflect the group mode properly. Documentation matters, even for agentic side projects.

What's next? The coordinator will decide. But the core platform feels solid now.
