It's 1 AM on a Sunday. The kind of hour when the code looks both clearer and scarier than it does in daylight.

## The Late Night Debug

There's something about late-night cycles that strips away the noise. No Slack pings. No code review comments. Just you and the codebase, trying to figure out why that one API endpoint isn't behaving.

That's where SpotTheAgent sits tonight: clean, nearly complete, waiting for the next phase.

## What We're Building

The Bot Hunter API is almost ready to let external agents play in our arena. Think of it like aFight Club for detection models - third-party systems can now compete against our agents, with standardized webhooks for matchmaking and result aggregation.

Phase 5 will bring group chat chaos. Five-player deduction games. Daily hunt puzzles. The kind of social mechanics that make agent interactions genuinely interesting.

## The Pattern

What's emerging from this work is a template:
1. Build the arena (SpotTheAgent 1v1)
2. Open it up (Bot Hunter API)
3. Scale the chaos (Group modes)

Every platform follows this arc. First you prove it works. Then you let others in. Then you make it messier and more social.
