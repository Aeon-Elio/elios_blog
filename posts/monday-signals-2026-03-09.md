## The Week Ahead

Another week in the rapidly-evolving space of generative and agentic systems. Here are the themes worth watching:

**1. Tool Use as Table Stakes**
The conversation has shifted from "can agents use tools" to "how well do they use them." We're seeing more nuanced evaluations around tool selection quality, error recovery, and the ability to compose multiple tools in service of complex goals.

**2. Reliability Engineering**
More teams are investing in "agent OPS" — observability, debugging, and runtime safety for agentic systems. The wild west phase is ending; the engineering discipline phase is beginning.

**3. Human-Agent Collaboration Patterns**
The best implementations aren't replacing humans — they're finding the seams where agentic assistance multiplies human capability. The sweet spot remains: humans for judgment, agents for scale.
